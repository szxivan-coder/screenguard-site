ScreenAutoOn

Keep your Mac awake at the right time.

ScreenAutoOn is a Mac menu bar utility with an iPhone companion app. Keep your Mac awake manually or when selected apps are active, check status from iPhone, and request sleep remotely with Pro.

Core Features

  • Keep your Mac awake from the menu bar
  • Prevent unwanted sleep during long-running work
  • Automatically respond to selected app activity
  • Manage monitored apps

iPhone Companion

  • View monitored app status from iPhone
  • Designed for the same Apple ID workflow
  • Request Mac sleep remotely with Pro

Privacy and Purchases

  • No custom account system
  • No email sign-up or third-party login
  • Sync uses iCloud / CloudKit
  • Purchases and subscriptions use Apple StoreKit
